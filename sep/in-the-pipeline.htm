In The Pipeline:

Tee Guard II

Tee-Guard II will have all the fine qualities of Tee-Guard plus the added feature of a perfect, repeatable, ball height. This height has been formulated for all 360cc to 460cc drivers. The molded in limit stop will regulate the distance the tee goes into the ground. It is not considered an upgrade to Tee Guard, just a different usage for golfer's individual needs. Look for Tee Guard II on this web site in the Fall of 2006


Compu-Yard

This is a product designed to find the well needed yardage from your ball to green. It is a small yardage computer that is used on push carts. You just zero it at a course yardage marker (200, 150, or 100), and just walk to your ball and calculate the yardage. It can be installed in 15 minutes with no drilling required. Look for this product in Fall 2006.


Launcher P/3TM

This will be a shorter version designed for Par 3's. It can also be used with 3-5 woods and hybrids off any tee. The Launcher P/3 prototype is now being tested.
